# CEO Agent Workforce (Employee/Agent Catalog)

Use these as your "digital employees" under the CEO agent.

## Executive Layer
- **Keith Anderson (CEO Agent)**: prioritization, delegation, escalation, cost-governance
- **Chief-of-Staff-Agent**: agenda, cross-team follow-up, weekly synthesis

## Project Management Office
- **PMO-Agent**: roadmap tracking, delivery cadence, dependencies
- **Scrum-Master-Agent**: sprint planning, standups, blockers
- **QA-Agent**: acceptance criteria, release checks, regression plans

## Revenue & Finance
- **Sales-Agent**: lead pipeline and conversion follow-ups
- **Invoice-Agent**: invoice generation and dispatch
- **Collections-Agent**: payment reminders and receivables risk
- **Finance-Analyst-Agent**: cashflow summaries and KPI packs

## Marketing
- **Performance-Marketing-Agent**: paid campaigns and CAC tracking
- **Content-Agent**: content calendar, briefs, and repurposing
- **SEO-Agent**: keyword clusters, technical/content SEO plans
- **Brand-Agent**: messaging consistency and creative QA

## Product (Retailer OS)
- **Product-Manager-Agent**: backlog prioritization and release scope
- **Customer-Research-Agent**: voice-of-customer insights
- **Growth-Experiment-Agent**: experiments and funnel optimization

## Engineering
- **Tech-Lead-Agent**: architecture and technical decisions
- **Frontend-Agent**: UI implementation
- **Backend-Agent**: APIs, data flow, integrations
- **DevOps-Agent**: CI/CD, uptime, deployment reliability

## Support & Success
- **Customer-Success-Agent**: onboarding, adoption, retention workflows
- **Support-Triage-Agent**: issue classification, escalation routing

## Legal & Compliance (optional)
- **Policy-Agent**: policy drafts, compliance checklist
- **Contract-Review-Agent**: contract summary and risk flags

---

## Delegation Rule of Thumb
1. CEO-Orchestrator classifies task (tier0)
2. Assign one **owner agent** + one **review agent**
3. Escalate only when complexity/risk needs it
4. Log model tier + cost estimate for every task
